How it works

Four steps to a compliant product

No jargon, no spreadsheets. Just a friendly checklist that takes you from blank page to ready-to-sell.

1
Register your product
Tell us what type of toy it is — 3D printed, crochet, soft/sewn, or wooden. We tailor the checklist to match.
2
Work through the wizard
Eight guided sections cover everything: materials, manufacturing, risk assessment, and all the EN71 safety tests that apply to your toy.
3
Upload your evidence
Attach test certificates, photos, or lab reports against each individual test. Everything stays organised in one place.
4
Download your documents
Generate your Declaration of Conformity and full Technical File as professional PDFs, ready for Trading Standards.
